Some words on the app

This app provides downloads of 'Newshour' news podcasts. It has been written by the programmer without any comercial interest and is meant to be useful for anyone who likes to listen to the "BBC Newshour" program. The programmer is not an employeee nor in any other form affiliated to the publisher of the podcasts.

Usage

The downloaded podcasts are stored by default in a subfolder "BBCWorldServicePodcasts" of the root folder of the internal storage of the device. Each podcast has a size of approx 22 MB (44 if you choose higher download quality). So it is a good idea to remove all downloaded podcasts from time to time in order to save storage space and to avoid a filled up storage.
